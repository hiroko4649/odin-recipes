Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of popular italian dishes which is loved by people in the world.

It is easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna 1 9 pieces
  • Canned tomatos
  • Prepared white source
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna
  2. While cooking lasagna, cook garlic and tomato in a pot
  3. Layer lasagna, tomato source, white source, and cheese
  4. Cook the dish in the oven for 30 min
